# crashes-persistence.kb — maintenance guide

Analyses that mine **crash records and persistence mechanisms** — what crashed
and when (to date/identify the malware) and what keeps junk resident (boot
receivers, jobs, alarms). See `../CLAUDE.md` for the shared schema and lifecycle.

Belongs here: crash-timeline and persistence analyses. Not here: install-time
reasoning (→ `../install-timeline.kb/`).
